Base





Base form . Goku spends the majority of his time throughout each Dragon Ball series in this form, his natural-born state. However, in base form, he is at his lowest level of power. In an important fight, Goku often discounts this form in order to transform into something far more powerful. That doesn't mean he still isn't a force to be reckoned with..

SSJ

Goku (Super Saiyan), often referred to as SS Goku or SSJ Goku, is DBFZ's resident shoto and an excellent, yet outclassed swiss army knife in a game full of swiss army knives. Very simple to pick up and effective at nearly every level, SS Goku has solid tools for almost every situation. He has great normals, good pressure and comboable beams









SSJ2

The interesting thing about Goku and SSJ2 is that this is the only time he didn’t get a new Saiyan transformation first. He was the first to get SSJ1, SSJ3, SSG, SSB and Ultra Instinct. It marks a brief period when Goku wasn’t the king of the universe, and Gohan was stronger for a short period until Goku would play catch up.








SSJ3

Super Saiyan 3 is, undoubtedly, an extremely powerful form that allows a Saiyan to fight at the highest possible power output. However, it is actually not an ideal state to enter at all. From what we know, Super Saiyan 3 is said to be a form that maximizes the ki output of a user, which is why the state appears to be so powerful.

Goku was the first Saiyan to achieve this form.He achieved this form first time when he have to fight with Beerus to save Earth.He achieved this form through a ritual not by training.









SSJ BLUE

First appearing in Dragon Ball Z: Resurrection 'F', Super Saiyan Blue has become one of the dominant new forms in the anime franchise, crafting a non-linear transformation that stacks multiple separate forms together into one epic standard of power.
